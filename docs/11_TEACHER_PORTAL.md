# Purpose

This document defines the complete Teacher Portal architecture of TEC.ERP.

The Teacher Portal is the operational center for instructors.

It allows professors to prepare classes, monitor cohorts, analyse learning progression, validate competencies and manage certifications.

The portal is designed to reduce administrative effort while increasing pedagogical quality.

---

# Teacher Philosophy

The professor is not merely an evaluator.

The professor acts as:

- Business Mentor
- ERP Coach
- Learning Facilitator
- Process Expert
- Decision Reviewer

The platform supports these responsibilities.

# Intervention Center

The system identifies students requiring attention.

Indicators include:

- low scores
- repeated mission failures
- incomplete modules
- weak competency progression
- certification risk

The professor may:

- add notes
- recommend review
- schedule follow-up
- assign additional practice

# AI Professor Assistant

The Teacher Portal includes AI support.

Examples:

- summarize cohort performance;
- identify common misconceptions;
- suggest classroom discussion topics;
- recommend review activities;
- highlight students requiring intervention.

AI suggestions never replace professor judgement.

---

# Version 1 Scope

Version 1 includes:

- dashboard
- cohort management
- student profiles
- mission review
- analytics
- certification monitoring
- reports
- AI insights

Out of scope:

- automated grading without rules;
- direct AI certification approval;
- institutional HR integration.

---

# Final Teacher Statement

The Teacher Portal exists to give instructors complete visibility of the learning journey.

It transforms classroom information into actionable pedagogical insight while preserving the professor as the central decision-maker.
