# TEC.ERP — Certification Framework

**Document:** Certification Framework  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Project:** TEC.ERP — Analyste ERP et Processus d'Affaires

---

# Purpose

This document defines the complete certification architecture of TEC.ERP.

Certification is not merely the result of completed modules.

It represents demonstrated professional competency.

Students earn certifications by progressively developing business reasoning, ERP process understanding and decision-making capability.

---

# Certification Philosophy

TEC.ERP certifications validate competencies.

They do not validate software memorization.

Students are evaluated through Business Missions, integrated processes, KPI interpretation and professional recommendations.

Certification should reflect workplace readiness.

---

# Certification Levels

Version 1 includes two institutional certifications.

- Silver Certification
- Gold Certification

Both certifications are publicly verifiable.

Both remain permanently associated with the student.

---

# Silver Certification

## Purpose

Silver validates ERP Foundations.

The student demonstrates understanding of:

- ERP concepts
- business departments
- organizational structure
- process integration
- master data
- data quality
- ERP readiness

Silver confirms that the student is prepared to begin operational ERP processes.

---

## Silver Requirements

The student must complete:

- Module 1
- Module 2

Requirements include:

- all Business Missions completed
- module assessments completed
- minimum certification score
- required competency level achieved

---

## Silver Competencies

Silver validates:

- business understanding
- ERP vocabulary
- organizational reasoning
- process mapping
- master data discipline
- readiness analysis

---

## Silver Deliverables

Upon completion:

- institutional certificate
- certificate ID
- QR verification
- public verification portal
- LinkedIn credential
- certification registry entry

---

# Gold Certification

## Purpose

Gold validates integrated ERP capability.

The student demonstrates the ability to connect departments, execute business processes, analyse KPIs and recommend business improvements.

---

## Gold Requirements

Prerequisite:

Silver Certification.

The student must complete:

- Modules 3 to 10
- Capstone
- Final recommendation

Requirements include:

- all Business Missions completed
- minimum average score
- competency validation
- integrated recommendation approved

---

## Gold Competencies

Gold validates:

- Procure-to-Pay
- Order-to-Cash
- Supply Chain
- Finance
- CRM
- Governance
- KPI Analysis
- AI-assisted reasoning
- Integrated ERP thinking

---

## Gold Deliverables

Upon completion:

- institutional certificate
- certificate ID
- QR verification
- public verification portal
- LinkedIn credential
- certification registry entry

---

# Certification Progress

Students should always see:

- current certification level
- completed modules
- remaining requirements
- average score
- competency progress
- estimated readiness

---

# Certification Rules

Certification depends on:

- completed Business Missions
- module completion
- competency achievement
- assessment completion
- Capstone completion (Gold)

Certification is never granted solely by attendance.

---

# Certificate Components

Each certificate contains:

- student name
- institution
- program
- certification level
- issue date
- certificate ID
- QR verification
- verification URL
- institutional signature
- platform seal

---

# Public Verification

Every certificate receives:

- unique certificate ID
- permanent verification URL
- QR code
- verification status

The verification page should display:

- certificate validity
- student name
- certification level
- issue date
- institution
- program
- verification status

---

# LinkedIn Integration

Students should be able to add certifications directly to LinkedIn.

The platform prepares:

- certification name
- institution
- issue date
- credential ID
- credential URL

---

# Professor Responsibilities

The professor may:

- monitor certification progress
- review competency evidence
- validate Capstone recommendations
- identify students needing intervention

The professor does not manually calculate certification.

The platform applies certification rules automatically.

---

# Administrative Responsibilities

Administrators manage:

- certificate templates
- institutional signatures
- verification registry
- certificate revocation (if required)
- cohort certification reports

---

# Certification Analytics

The platform should monitor:

- Silver completion rate
- Gold completion rate
- average certification score
- module contribution
- cohort comparison
- certification trends

---

# Version 1 Scope

Version 1 includes:

- Silver Certification
- Gold Certification
- automatic eligibility validation
- certificate generation
- QR verification
- public verification portal
- LinkedIn integration

Out of scope:

- continuing education credits
- digital badges
- blockchain verification
- third-party certification exchange

---

# Final Certification Statement

TEC.ERP certifications recognize demonstrated professional capability.

They certify that the learner has successfully applied ERP concepts, business reasoning and integrated process understanding through realistic Business Missions and measurable competency development.
