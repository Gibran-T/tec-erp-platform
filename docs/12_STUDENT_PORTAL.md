# Purpose

This document defines the Student Portal architecture of TEC.ERP.

The Student Portal is the primary learning environment.

It transforms classroom content into an interactive ERP experience where students progressively develop professional competencies through Business Missions.

The portal should be intuitive, motivating, professional and focused on learning outcomes.

---

# Student Philosophy

The student is not a software operator.

The student acts as a Junior ERP Business Analyst.

Every interaction should reinforce:

- business understanding;
- process reasoning;
- professional decision-making;
- continuous improvement.

---

# Student Home

The Home page answers immediately:

- Where am I?
- What should I do next?
- What have I already completed?
- What is my certification progress?
- What requires my attention today?

- Home

- Modules

- Resources

---

# Mission Control

Mission Control is the central learning workspace.

Students can:

- start missions;
- continue missions;
- review completed missions;
- see mission progress;
- access mission feedback;
- monitor certification progress.

---

# Module Experience

Each module contains:

- learning objective;
- business context;
- three Business Missions;
- dashboard;
- reflection;
- module completion.

Modules unlock progressively.

---

# Business Mission Experience

Each mission follows the same flow.

## Business Context

Explains the company situation.

## Objective

Defines what must be achieved.

## Input Information

Provides all business information required.

## ERP Activity

Student performs the required ERP activity.

## Decision

Student makes a business decision.

## Consequences

System displays business impact.

## Feedback

Student receives structured feedback.

## Completion

Mission score and next recommendation.

---

# Progress Tracking

Students should always see:

- module progress;
- mission progress;
- average score;
- competencies achieved;
- certification readiness;
- completed milestones.

---

# Personal Dashboard

The Student Dashboard displays:

- current module;
- current mission;
- completed missions;
- average score;
- competency progression;
- certification status;
- recent feedback;
- AI recommendations.

---

# Learning Timeline

Students can review their complete learning history.

Timeline includes:

- mission completion;
- quiz completion;
- certifications;
- AI coaching sessions;
- feedback history.

---

# AI Coach Access

Students may access AI Coach from every mission.

The AI Coach can:

- explain concepts;
- clarify consequences;
- ask reflection questions;
- suggest review topics;
- reinforce business reasoning.

The AI Coach never completes missions for the student.

---

# Business Dashboard

Students may review business KPIs generated during simulations.

Examples:

- ERP Readiness
- Data Quality
- Inventory Availability
- Procurement Performance
- Sales Performance
- Customer Service
- Financial Impact

The dashboard demonstrates how business decisions affect organizational performance.

---

# Certification Center

Students can monitor:

## Silver Progress

- competency achievement;
- remaining requirements.

## Gold Progress

- module completion;
- Capstone readiness;
- recommendation status;
- final eligibility.

# Student Profile

---

# Accessibility

The Student Portal should follow accessibility best practices.

Requirements include:

- keyboard navigation;
- screen reader compatibility;
- high contrast support;
- responsive layouts;
- readable typography;
- consistent navigation.

---

# Mobile Experience

Version 1 prioritizes desktop learning.

Mobile support focuses on:

- dashboard review;
- certification verification;
- feedback consultation;
- progress tracking.

Business Missions remain optimized for desktop.

---

# Motivation System

The platform encourages continuous progress through:

- milestone indicators;
- module completion;
- certification progress;
- competency evolution;
- AI encouragement;
- visible achievements.

Motivation should remain professional.

Gamification should never reduce institutional credibility.

---

# Student Privacy

Students only access:

- their own data;
- their own missions;
- their own dashboards;
- their own certifications.

No cohort comparison exposing individual classmates should be displayed.

---

# Version 1 Scope

Version 1 includes:

- Mission Control;
- Module navigation;
- Business Missions;
- dashboards;
- AI Coach;
- certification tracking;
- resources;
- personal profile.

Out of scope:

- social networking;
- student messaging;
- peer grading;
- collaborative simulations.

---

# Final Student Statement

The Student Portal exists to transform ERP education into an authentic business experience.

Students progressively evolve from beginners into professionals capable of understanding enterprise processes, analysing business situations and making informed ERP decisions.
