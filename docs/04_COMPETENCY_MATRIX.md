# Purpose

The Competency Matrix defines the professional competencies developed throughout the TEC.ERP program.

Rather than measuring only completed activities, TEC.ERP evaluates what the student is capable of understanding, analysing, executing and recommending after each module.

The matrix connects learning outcomes, business missions, assessments and certifications.

---

# Competency Framework

Each module develops competencies in four dimensions:

1. Business Understanding
2. Process Execution
3. Analytical Thinking
4. Professional Decision-Making

# Assessment Philosophy

Competencies are demonstrated through Business Missions.

Students are evaluated by their reasoning, decisions and understanding of business consequences, not by memorizing ERP transactions.
