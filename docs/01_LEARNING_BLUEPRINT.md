# TEC.ERP — Learning Blueprint

**Document:** Learning Blueprint  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Project:** TEC.ERP — Analyste ERP et Processus d'Affaires  

---

# Purpose

This document defines the complete learning journey of TEC.ERP.

It describes how the student progresses through ten modules, thirty business missions, two certification levels and one continuous company story.

---

# Learning Blueprint Structure

## LB-01 — Purpose

Defines why this document exists and how it guides the design of the course.

## LB-02 — Learning Model

Defines how the student learns ERP through business missions, decisions, consequences and reflection.

## LB-03 — Course Structure

Defines the 10-module structure, the 30-hour format and the post-course access model.

## LB-04 — Certification Structure

Defines the Silver and Gold certification checkpoints.

## LB-05 — Standard Class Flow

Defines the standard rhythm of each class session.

## LB-06 — M1 ERP Foundations

Defines the first module, focused on ERP awareness, company understanding and readiness diagnosis.

## LB-07 — M2 Master Data & Organizational Structure

Defines the second module, focused on organizational structure, master data and data quality.

## LB-08 — M3 Procure-to-Pay

Defines the third module, focused on the purchasing cycle.

## LB-09 — M4 Order-to-Cash

Defines the fourth module, focused on the sales cycle.

## LB-10 — M5 Supply Chain & Inventory

Defines the fifth module, focused on supply chain visibility, inventory decisions and replenishment.

## LB-11 — M6 Finance & Accounting

Defines the sixth module, focused on the financial impact of ERP transactions.

## LB-12 — M7 CRM & Customer Service

Defines the seventh module, focused on customer relationships and service management.

## LB-13 — M8 Human Resources & Governance

Defines the eighth module, focused on people, roles, approvals and governance.

## LB-14 — M9 Business Intelligence, KPI & AI

Defines the ninth module, focused on dashboards, KPIs, analysis and AI-assisted recommendations.

## LB-15 — M10 ERP Capstone & Final Certification

Defines the final integrated module and Gold certification challenge.

## LB-16 — Professor Experience

Defines how the professor facilitates the learning journey.

## LB-17 — Student Experience

Defines how the student experiences the platform and evolves as a Junior ERP Analyst.

## LB-18 — Post-Course Learning

Defines how the student continues learning during the 90-day access period.

## LB-19 — Final Learning Statement

Summarizes the educational purpose of the TEC.ERP learning journey.

# Working Method

This document will be completed section by section.

Each section must be validated before implementation begins.

No module should be implemented before its corresponding Learning Blueprint section is approved.
