---

# Purpose

This document defines the implementation roadmap of TEC.ERP.

It translates the Product Vision into a structured execution plan, ensuring that development follows educational priorities before technical complexity.

The roadmap establishes milestones, implementation phases and architectural priorities for Version 1.

---

# Development Philosophy

TEC.ERP is developed as a product.

Every feature must satisfy three conditions:

- educational value;
- architectural consistency;
- long-term maintainability.

The platform grows incrementally.

Every iteration must leave the system in a deployable state.

---

# Guiding Principles

Development follows these principles:

- Business First
- Learning First
- Mission First
- Modular Development
- Continuous Validation
- Small Deliveries
- Production Quality
- Institutional Reliability
