# Purpose

This document defines the educational philosophy that guides every learning experience within TEC.ERP.

It establishes the pedagogical principles that influence the design of every module, business mission, simulation, dashboard, assessment and certification.

The objective is not to teach a specific ERP software.

The objective is to develop professionals capable of understanding, integrating and improving business processes through ERP systems.

---

# Core Learning Principles

## LP-001 — Learning through Business Missions

Students never begin with transactions.

They begin with a business problem.

Every activity must have a realistic business objective.

---

## LP-002 — Context Before Action

Every action must begin with understanding.

Students first analyse the situation.

Only afterwards do they interact with the system.

---

## LP-003 — Processes Before Software

Students learn business processes.

Software becomes the environment where those processes are executed.

The process always comes before the interface.

---

## LP-004 — Decisions Before Validation

The system should encourage reasoning.

Students make decisions before receiving feedback.

Learning occurs through reflection rather than memorization.

---

## LP-005 — Learning through Consequences

Every business decision creates measurable consequences.

Students observe the operational and managerial impact of their choices.

---

## LP-006 — Progressive Complexity

The platform evolves together with the learner.

Each module introduces new responsibilities, new information and new business challenges.

---

## LP-007 — AI as Learning Coach

Artificial Intelligence supports learning.

It explains.

It questions.

It guides.

It never replaces the student's reasoning.

---

## LP-008 — One Company, One Story

The complete learning journey occurs inside the same company.

Students progressively transform the organisation throughout the ten modules.

---

## LP-009 — Competency First

Success is measured by demonstrated competencies.

Completing activities is not enough.

Students must demonstrate understanding and decision-making capability.

---

## LP-010 — Continuous Learning

Learning does not end after thirty classroom hours.

The platform remains available for review, practice and professional development.

---

# Educational Goal

The objective of TEC.ERP is to transform learners into ERP professionals capable of understanding organisations, integrating departments, analysing business processes and supporting digital transformation initiatives.
