# TEC.ERP Three-Mission Pedagogical Contract

> **Status:** CONTRACT — NOT IMPLEMENTED · Wave 1 Learning Architecture · Do not treat as shipped UX.

**Authority:** TEC.ERP Learning Operating System Master Contract · Owner review required before Wave 2.

---

Each V2 module has **exactly three** regular missions with **distinct pedagogical roles**. Three similar tasks are forbidden.

## Mission 1 — COMPRENDRE ET CADRER

Purpose: identify business situation; understand actors and data; recognize process boundaries; explain business rule; establish context.

## Mission 2 — EXÉCUTER ET CONTRÔLER

Purpose: perform ERP operations; create or validate documents; follow sequence; apply controls; produce evidence.

## Mission 3 — ANALYSER, RÉCUPÉRER ET DÉCIDER

Purpose: diagnose exception; interpret KPI; recover process; justify decision; explain downstream impact.

## Required fields per mission

Each mission definition MUST include:

objective · scenario · learner role · starting condition · evidence · ERP action · document · control · risk · expected decision · possible errors · recovery path · AI support boundary · BI connection · competency evidence · completion criteria · debrief

## AI support boundary (all missions)

AI MAY clarify, question, structure reasoning, and request evidence.  
AI MUST NOT submit missions, unlock progression, change scores, or reveal answer keys.

## BI connection

Mission 1 may preview KPI context; Mission 3 MUST connect to interpretation/recovery; module ANALYSER phase consolidates BI Studio work.
