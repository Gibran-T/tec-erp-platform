# TEC.ERP Content and Pedagogical Quality Standard

> **Status:** CONTRACT — NOT IMPLEMENTED · Wave 1 Learning Architecture · Do not treat as shipped UX.

**Authority:** TEC.ERP Learning Operating System Master Contract · Owner review required before Wave 2.

---

## Forbidden

- walls of text  
- generic filler  
- repeated competency sentence on every card  
- identical module template without module identity  
- raw enums as primary content  
- technical IDs as primary content  
- unexplained acronyms  
- BI without interpretation  
- AI without evidence  
- mission without business consequence  
- slide without teaching purpose  
- image without learning purpose  
- Professor view without a decision  
- learner page without a next step  
- mandate opening with theory wall  
- mode-ambiguous activities  
- AI-only Twin mutation  
- KPI movement without cause  
- unlabeled forecasts  
- silent important actions  
- reflection without reapplication  
- points-only success criteria  

## Required

Module identity · process consequence · evidence link · next step CTA · FR/EN readiness · V1/V2-aware copy · explicit experience mode · mandat framing · debrief + reapplication for major simulations · Visible vs Ambient AI distinction where both appear
