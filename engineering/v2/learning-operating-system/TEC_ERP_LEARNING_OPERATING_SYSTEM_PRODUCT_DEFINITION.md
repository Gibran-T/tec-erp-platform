## Official product statement

**TEC.ERP is a Learning Operating System** that combines, in one coherent product:

1. Academic portal  
2. Professional ERP simulator  
3. Digital classroom  
4. Process laboratory  
5. BI Studio  
6. AI Decision Workspace  
7. Teaching Deck  
8. Professor Presentation Mode  
9. Competency evidence  
10. Assessments  
11. Capstone (separate integrator)  
12. Evidence-based certification  

TEC.ERP teaches enterprise work by placing the learner inside business situations that require analysis, execution, control, recovery, interpretation, reflection, and communication — not by listing modules or hosting manuals.

## What TEC.ERP is not

TEC.ERP SHALL NOT be defined or designed as:

- a module list or app launcher  
- a content repository or long-form manual  
- a generic LMS  
- a dashboard collection  
- a simple ERP clone  
- a chatbot attached to missions  

## Product promise

Learners progress through **M1–M10** (exactly ten competency modules), **30 regular missions** (three per module with distinct pedagogical roles), integrated **BI** and **AI** reasoning spaces, a separate **Capstone**, and **Gold certification** issued on evidence + Professor approval (version-aware).

Professors teach from the platform using Teaching Decks, ERP demonstration, anonymized evidence, BI, AI supervision, assessment correction, Capstone review, and Gold issuance when eligible.
