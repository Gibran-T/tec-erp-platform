# TEC.ERP BI Studio Learning Contract

> **Status:** CONTRACT — NOT IMPLEMENTED · Wave 1 Learning Architecture · Do not treat as shipped UX.

**Authority:** TEC.ERP Learning Operating System Master Contract · Owner review required before Wave 2.

---

**Do not implement BI Studio in this wave.**

BI Studio is a pedagogical decision environment inspired by professional BI practice — not a chart gallery.

## Required elements

filters · KPI cards · target vs actual · trend · variance · semantic signals · charts · drill-down · process source · documents · root causes · risks · recommendations · learner analysis area · Professor correction mode

## KPI definition (binding)

Every KPI MUST define: business definition · formula · unit · period · target · actual · variance · threshold logic · source data · process owner · business meaning · risk · recommended action · evidence links

## Learning sequence

1 observe · 2 filter · 3 compare · 4 explain · 5 diagnose · 6 recommend · 7 measure recovery

## Learner questions (mandatory)

What happened? Why? Which process caused it? What evidence proves it? What decision is recommended? How will recovery be measured?

## Non-goals

No decorative charts without interpretation · No KPI without owner/formula · No AI fabricating KPI facts
