# TEC.ERP Research Reference Canon

> **Status:** CONTRACT — NOT IMPLEMENTED · Wave 1A Research Addendum · Do not treat as shipped UX.

**Authority:** TEC.ERP Learning Operating System Master Contract · Owner review required before Wave 2.

---

## Purpose

This canon is the authoritative research and design tradition map for the Learning Operating System. It extracts **principles**, not proprietary screens, copy, or assets. Every major Learning OS decision SHOULD cite a row in this canon.

## How to use this canon

1. Before designing a surface, mode, or simulation mechanic, identify the governing tradition(s).  
2. Apply the **TEC.ERP application** column; respect **prohibited misuse**.  
3. Map delivery to the **related future wave**.  
4. Prefer internal consistency with Digital Twin, experience modes, and Professor centrality over novelty.

## Non-goals

- No copying of proprietary SAP, Microsoft, Harvard, MIT, Wharton, IBM, or other vendor/school UI, content, cases, or media.  
- No claiming institutional affiliation with external schools or vendors.  
- No substituting research labels for pedagogical substance.
