## Purpose

The learner must see **two journeys simultaneously**: how the company evolves, and how their professional identity evolves.

## Enterprise journey

company discovery → data foundation → procurement → sales → inventory → finance → customer → workforce → governance → BI and consulting

## Professional journey

observer → junior analyst → process analyst → ERP consultant → trusted advisor

- Where they are
